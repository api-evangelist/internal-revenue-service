# Internal Revenue Service (internal-revenue-service)
The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) is the United States federal tax collection agency and a bureau of the Department of the Treasury. The IRS provides data access and developer resources for tax-related information and e-filing integration.

### IRS e-File
The IRS provides APIs and resources for electronic tax filing, allowing tax software providers and developers to integrate with IRS e-file systems for submitting tax returns electronically.
